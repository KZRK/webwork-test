Evaluating \(x^2+y^2 = 1\) for \(x = \frac{7}{10}\) gives

\begin{equation*} {(\frac{7}{10}})^2+y^2 = 1 \end{equation*}

Solving for y yields

\begin{equation*} y^2 = {(0.714142842854285)}^2 \end{equation*}

Since the point is in quadrant IV, then y is negative and so \(y = -0.714142842854285\text{.}\)
