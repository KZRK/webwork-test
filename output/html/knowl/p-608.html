  1. \(\displaystyle \log_{6}{(12)} + \log_{6}{(18)}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \log_8(x) + \log_8(x - 12)\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \log_{5}{(2500)} - \log_{5}{(4)}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \log(5b) + \log(2c^2)\)

  5. \(\displaystyle \log_{25}{(7)} + \log_5{(3)}\)

  6. Sketch the graph of \(h(x)=1-5\log{(1-\frac{x}{2})}\text{.}\)
