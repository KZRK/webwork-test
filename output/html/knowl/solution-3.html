Solution 1.0.16.1.

To subtract two fractions with the same denominator, we simply subtract the numerators and keep the denominator unchanged.

\(\displaystyle{\begin{aligned} \frac{7}{12} - \frac{17}{12} \amp = \frac{7 - 17}{12} \\ \amp = \frac{-10}{12} \amp \hbox{We can reduce this by dividing 2 into both the numerator and the denominator. }\\ \amp = -\frac{5}{6} \\ \end{aligned} }\)
